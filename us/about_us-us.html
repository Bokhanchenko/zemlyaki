Creators:


In 1987, in response to the terrible tragedy of the ecological group of Pripyat - Enthusiast Club was founded "Zemlyaki", which in a few years in officially registered public charity. Today "Zemlyaki" unite more than a thousand members, which operate activists - about 15 volunteers.


We help:


In Desnyanskiy district of Kiev were evacuated in 1986, more than 40,000 displaced persons from the town of Pripyat, Chernobyl and settlements in the 30 km exclusion zone. These people are crippled destinies irrevocably poor health, with sick children in their arms constantly fighting for survival in the state, indifferent to their problems and troubles. Our organization provides assistance to evacuees, the liquidators of the Chernobyl consequences, large families, as well as widows and orphans, the disabled, which every day more and more.

Social support:


For the most needy families: mothers with many children, orphans, widows, disabled persons living below the poverty line or in the critical situation created and the program works «SOS», which has such people financial and material support. People come to us with their problems: no money for medicine, there is no financial ability to do the surgery, nothing to bury relatives, etc. For children with disabilities, victims of the Chernobyl disaster has developed a program "Child victims of Chernobyl" .Deti born to parents evacuees are born with the disease. For financial support of these programs the organization is always looking for sponsors. For members of the organization are constantly working amateur associations, folk art, mugs for the study of foreign languages: German, Japanese, English Adults and children with great pleasure and interest to attend classes. Work permanent exhibitions of works by artists, florists amateur masters of needlework.
