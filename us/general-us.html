Society of Disabled Chernobyl "Zemlyaki"

Our mission:


The moral and psychological, medical, recreational, social and material assistance to the victims of the Chernobyl disaster 26 April 1986, as well as provide comprehensive assistance in the development and implementation of creative abilities, talents, support for widows, orphans, the disabled Chernobyl.


A bit of history:


The accident at the Chernobyl nuclear power plant, recognized by the international community, the most ambitious technological and environmental disasters in the history of mankind on Earth.

The explosion at Unit 4 occurred with the release of large amounts of radioactive substances into the environment and has made more than 50 million curies, which is equivalent to the effects of the explosion of more than 500 atomic bombs dropped in 1945 on the Japanese city of Hiroshima.

City - satellite Chernobyl - Pripyat - has become the epicenter of the Chernobyl disaster. 26-27 April 1986 pripyatchanin received each dose of 20 000 times greater than the rate of accidental exposure to the public, adopted in the Soviet Union before Chernobyl and 95 000 times more than the norm adopted in the United States and Europe. So far in the distant future (over 300 years) Pripyat remains the most polluted area in the 30-km zone of the Chernobyl accident.


Creators:


In 1987, in response to the terrible tragedy of the ecological group of Pripyat - Enthusiast Club was founded "Zemlyaki", which in a few years in officially registered public charity. Today "Zemlyaki" unite more than a thousand members, which operate activists - about 15 volunteers.


We help:


In Desnyanskiy district of Kiev were evacuated in 1986, more than 40,000 displaced people from the town of Pripyat, Chernobyl and settlements in the 30 km exclusion zone. These people are crippled destinies irrevocably poor health, with sick children in their arms constantly fighting for survival in the state, indifferent to their problems and troubles.

Our organization provides assistance to evacuees, the liquidators of the Chernobyl consequences, large families, as well as widows and orphans, the disabled, which every day more and more.
