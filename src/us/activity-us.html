The program «SOS»


After the experienced disaster, suffering a stress from the loss of his home and family, as well as under the pressure of environmental and socio-economic crisis in the country most vulnerable populations are in a critical physical and psychological condition, and many - and a difficult financial situation.

Every year, deteriorating health status of these people, a growing number of cancers, increases the mortality rate, which means - an increasing number of widows, orphans, lonely people.

For Needy Families program works created and «SOS», which provides comprehensive support to such people. People come to us with their problems: no money for medicine, there is no financial ability to do the surgery, nothing to bury relatives, etc.
